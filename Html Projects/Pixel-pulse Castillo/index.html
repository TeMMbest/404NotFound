About Me & PixelPulse

I'm Selwyn Castillo, an aspiring developer currently working at PixelPulse, where I'm learning to create innovative digital solutions (and occasionally break them). At PixelPulse, I'm part of a dynamic team that transforms ideas into pixel-perfect realities, or at least pixel-okay realities when the deadline is tomorrow.

Working at PixelPulse has given me the opportunity to develop three core competencies: problem-solving (mostly by googling "why is my code not working"), technical versatility (I can write "Hello World" in 7 different languages), and team collaboration (I'm great at asking "can someone help me debug this?"). Every day brings new learning opportunities, usually in the form of error messages I've never seen before.

PixelPulse Philosophy: "Every pixel matters, every line of code tells a story, and every bug is just a feature waiting to be discovered."
